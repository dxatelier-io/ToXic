CHAPTER I

You Were Beautiful That Day

You were beautiful that day, and i fell in love.

Our first meeting was brief, almost forgettable by ordinary standards. And yet, your face refused to fade the way moments usually do. I thought the feeling would dissolve with time, that distance would do what logic always promises. It didn’t.

With each passing day, there was always something about you that pulled me deeper. A detail. A gesture. A thought i couldn’t unsee. That was when it crossed my mind, "i am in danger."

Because i knew this pattern too well. I knew what it meant when i began placing my heart in someone’s hands. I knew i was starting to lose myself again.

Foolishly, instead of stopping, i stayed. Worse, i leaned in. This cursed heart of mine only grew more attached to you, as if it had never learned how to leave.
